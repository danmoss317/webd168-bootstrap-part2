About Me

I was born on St. Patrick's Day in 1989, and grew up in the city of Corona in Southern California. I spent the first 26 years of my life there before moving to Riverside, California. After seven months, I moved to San Diego in 2016 and still reside here today.

In my spare time I enjoy listening to music, playing video games, watching sports, riding my bicycle, golfing, and spending time with my girlfriend, family, and friends. I enjoy all kinds of different music, but one of my favorite things to do is to switch on the lava lamp and throw on a classic rock record. The video game I play the most right now is Rocket League on Xbox, but it's not uncommon for me to fire up the old Nintendo and get down on some 8-bit side-scrolling action. My favorite sport to watch is football, but I also enjoy basketball and soccer. My favorite football team is the Denver Broncos, for basketball I root for the Portland Trail Blazers, and the US National Team is my favorite when it comes to soccer.
